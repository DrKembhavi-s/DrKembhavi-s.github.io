## Published Books

### Who Is John Galt of Ayurveda?
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Publisher:** Amazon KDP / Pothi.com  
**Status:** <span class="badge published">Published</span>

A provocative inquiry into the crisis of leadership, identity, and intellectual courage in contemporary Ayurveda. Drawing on the Objectivist metaphor of a productive mind on strike, this book asks whether Ayurveda has abandoned its own first principles — and who, if anyone, is willing to reclaim them.

## Forthcoming Books

> These titles are completed or in final preparation for KDP / Pothi.com publication. Dates will be updated as they go live.

---

### Real Steel, Real Revival
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Status:** <span class="badge forthcoming">Forthcoming</span>

An unflinching examination of what a genuine revival of Ayurveda demands — not cosmetic modernisation, but structural, epistemic, and institutional transformation. The title is deliberately industrial: revival without real steel is decoration.

---

### Ayurveda at the Crossroads
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Status:** <span class="badge forthcoming">Forthcoming</span>

A critical analysis of the defining choices Ayurveda faces at the intersection of regulation, research culture, education reform, and global integration. Written from the inside — by a practitioner who has lived these contradictions.

---

### Ayurveda at the Epistemological Crossroads
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Status:** <span class="badge forthcoming">Forthcoming</span>

A deeper philosophical companion to *Ayurveda at the Crossroads*, interrogating how Ayurvedic knowledge is produced, validated, distorted, and transmitted — with particular attention to the LLM era and the risks of epistemic laundering.

---

### Assessing with Integrity
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Status:** <span class="badge forthcoming">Forthcoming</span>

A comprehensive guide to assessment philosophy and practice in Ayurvedic medical education. Addresses the gap between NCISM-mandated assessment frameworks and the lived reality of examination culture in Ayurveda colleges.

---

### Research Methodology and Biostatistics for Ayurveda PG Scholars
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Status:** <span class="badge forthcoming">Forthcoming</span>

An NCISM-aligned textbook written specifically for postgraduate scholars in Ayurveda. Bridges the persistent gap between generic research methodology texts and the conceptual and practical demands of Ayurvedic research design.

---

### Synopsis Writing Handbook for Ayurveda PG Scholars
**Author:** Dr. Aakash Kembhavi  
**Status:** <span class="badge forthcoming">Forthcoming</span>

A practical, step-by-step handbook for writing research synopses in Ayurveda PG programmes — addressing the specific requirements of RGUHS and other affiliating universities, with annotated examples and common error analysis.
