🌿 The Ayurvedic ecological vocabulary we have abandoned
Loka Purusha Samya Janapadodhvamsa Ritucharya Dinacharya Panchamahabhuta Vrikshayurveda Ojakshaya Vyadhikshamatva Dravyaguna conservation Swasthyasya Swasthya Rakshanam

Ayurveda's foundational texts encoded a sophisticated ecological ethic millennia before modern environmentalism. The health of the body and the health of the world are one question, not two.

"The forest we forgot was never just trees. It was the ecological memory of a civilisation that knew how to live within its means on this particular piece of earth, in these particular seasons, with these particular plants and rains and soils. Ayurveda was that memory's medical expression." — Dr. Aakash Kembhavi, The Forest We Forgot, Ayurveda Unfiltered, 2026
